About

Hello! I'm Trevor, a passionate software engineer who enjoys building things that everyone can use. I develop exceptional web and mobile applications that provide clean, intuitive user interfaces with efficient and modern backends.

Shortly after graduating from Portland State University, I travelled the world and took advantage of this time to take my software engineering skills to the next level.

My experience resides in a multitude of areas, including mobile and game development, backend development, and Ethereum blockchain development (Smart Contracts). I'm always willing to learn and adapt to new technologies and languages if need be.
